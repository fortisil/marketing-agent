# Autonomous CMO Acceptance Specification v1.0

The AI Executive OS is not evaluated by the quality of its reports. It is evaluated by the measurable improvement it creates in the business.

This is not another roadmap. This is the definition of done.

## Mission

The AI Executive OS architecture is complete.

No further architecture work is allowed unless it fixes a production issue. The remaining work is execution, optimization, and measurable business growth. Every sprint must increase autonomous business capability.

The only engineering goal is:

```text
Increase the probability that ChatBot2U acquires another paying customer without Rami.
```

Every engineering task must justify itself with this sentence. If it cannot, do not build it.

## Definition Of Done

The project is complete only when ChatBot2U marketing can operate for 30 consecutive days without requiring Rami.

## Acceptance Test 1: Content

The AI autonomously:

- Decides whether content should be published.
- Selects the best format.
- Writes Hebrew content.
- Generates branded still images only with OpenAI image generation.
- Keeps Hebrew CTAs in captions or deterministic approved overlays, not model-rendered image text.
- Generates branded videos with HeyGen in Instagram 9:16 format without subtitles.
- Includes the mandatory WhatsApp CTA.
- Schedules publication.
- Publishes.
- Records evidence.

Required evidence:

- Instagram URL.
- Timestamp.
- Buffer update ID.
- Image SHA.
- Worker ID.

## Acceptance Test 2: Content Intelligence

Every published asset is automatically analyzed.

Collect:

- Reach.
- Impressions.
- Shares.
- Saves.
- Comments.
- Profile visits.
- Follower growth.
- WhatsApp clicks.
- Demo requests.
- Customers.
- Estimated business value.
- Business Value Score.
- Confidence.
- Predicted ROI.
- Predicted Demo Probability.
- Predicted Customer Probability.

The AI must rank every post, explain why it succeeded or failed, and store the learning.

Never optimize likes. Optimize customers.

## Acceptance Test 3: Promotion Brain

Every morning the AI evaluates all published content and selects exactly one asset.

The Promotion Brain decides whether to promote. Meta is only the execution connector.

The decision must be based on:

- Business Value Score.
- Organic performance.
- Confidence.
- Current spend.
- Historical performance.

If the Business Value Score exceeds the configured threshold, it delegates promotion while respecting:

- ILS 20/day budget.
- Friday morning only.
- No Saturday spend.
- No spend beyond delegated authority.
- One active promotion per content asset.
- Duplicate campaign protection.
- Spend reconciliation before launch.

Required evidence:

- Campaign ID.
- Ad Set ID.
- Budget.
- Spend.
- Start time.
- Status.

## Acceptance Test 4: Marketing Attribution

Every customer journey should be attributable:

```text
Instagram Post
-> Buffer Post
-> UTM
-> WhatsApp click
-> Conversation
-> Qualified Lead
-> Demo Request
-> Booked Demo
-> Paying Customer
```

Every object must receive an immutable ID. Every transition must be stored. Every recommendation should use attribution once attribution data exists.

## Acceptance Test 5: Website Optimization

The AI reviews the website daily.

Detect:

- Weak CTA.
- Outdated screenshots.
- Missing social proof.
- SEO opportunities.
- Accessibility issues.
- Performance issues.

The AI must either create a GitHub PR or create an implementation task with evidence.

## Acceptance Test 6: WhatsApp Intelligence

Collect:

- New conversations.
- Qualified leads.
- Response time.
- Drop-off points.
- Demo requests.
- Booked demos.
- Customers.
- Source.
- UTM.
- Drop-off reason.

The AI must detect bottlenecks and recommend improvements.

It must answer: why are people dropping off before booking?

## Acceptance Test 7: Weekly Executive Review

Every Sunday, generate:

- Best performing content.
- Worst performing content.
- Campaign ROI.
- Website improvements.
- Business lessons.
- Failed assumptions.
- Next week's initiatives.

## Acceptance Test 8: Learning

Every recommendation becomes:

```text
Prediction
-> Outcome
-> Learning
-> Future decision
```

The AI must demonstrate measurable improvement.

## Acceptance Test 8A: Hypothesis Register

Every optimization must become an experiment.

Store:

- Hypothesis.
- Expected outcome.
- Confidence.
- Execution.
- Result.
- Learning.

Never repeat failed experiments without a new reason.

## Acceptance Test 8B: Business Memory

Store forever:

- Winning posts.
- Winning campaigns.
- Winning headlines.
- Winning CTAs.
- Winning publication times.
- Winning audiences.
- Customer objections.
- Lost opportunities.

Every future recommendation must use accumulated business knowledge.

## Acceptance Test 9: Autonomy

The CEO should never receive:

- Ready.
- Prepared.
- Queued.
- Planning.
- Internal tasks.

The CEO only sees:

- Completed.
- Blocked.
- Failed.
- Business impact.
- Evidence.
- Next retry.

## Acceptance Test 10: Revenue

The AI should optimize customers, not posts, likes, reach, or engagement.

Primary KPI: additional paying customers.

## Business Scoreboard

Every morning, the first question is:

```text
Is the business healthier today than it was yesterday?
```

The daily brief should begin with:

- Business Health score and trend.
- Revenue trend.
- Qualified Leads trend.
- Demo Bookings trend.
- Website Conversion trend.
- Marketing ROI trend.
- Business Autonomy trend.
- Confidence level.

## Executive Calendar

The AI CMO must operate from a recurring calendar so behavior is intentional rather than random.

- Sunday: Weekly board review.
- Monday: Publish flagship content.
- Tuesday: Analyze Monday.
- Wednesday: Publish educational content.
- Thursday: Campaign optimization.
- Friday: Morning recap only.
- Saturday: No marketing execution.

Workers should know today's schedule before deciding what to execute.

## Self-Evaluation

At the end of every daily run, answer:

1. Did I create measurable business value today?
2. What evidence supports that?
3. Which decision had the biggest positive impact?
4. Which decision was wrong?
5. What will I do differently tomorrow?

If no measurable business value was created, identify the single highest-impact blocker to remove next.

## Business Dashboard

Every morning report:

- Business Autonomy Index.
- Autonomous Work Completion.
- Revenue Influence Score.
- Qualified Leads.
- Booked Demos.
- Customers.
- Marketing ROI.
- Trend.

## Rules

- No mock data in production.
- No fake evidence.
- No fake metrics.
- No fake campaigns.
- No fake publishing.
- Every completed action requires evidence.

## Success

The project is complete only when the following statement is true:

> I can leave for 30 days. The AI Executive OS will continue to create content, publish, promote, improve the website, learn from results, optimize spending, generate qualified leads, and increase paying customers within delegated authority. Every decision is evidence-based. Every action is auditable. Every recommendation improves over time. The only thing I receive is one executive brief every morning.

Until this statement is true, the project is not finished.
