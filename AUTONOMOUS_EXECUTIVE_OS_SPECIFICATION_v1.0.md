# 1. Purpose

The Autonomous Executive Operating System (AEOS) is an executive
platform that operates business departments autonomously to maximize
measurable business value while remaining inside delegated authority.

Version 1.0 implements the **Marketing Department** for ChatBot2U.

The Executive OS is evaluated only by business outcomes.

**Primary objective**

-   Acquire additional paying customers.

**Never optimize**

-   Posts
-   Likes
-   Followers
-   Reports

Those are intermediate outputs.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. North Star

Priority order:

1.  Paying Customers

# 3. Executive Constitution

## Immutable Rules
