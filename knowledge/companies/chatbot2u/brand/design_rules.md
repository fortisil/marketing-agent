# ChatBot2U Design Rules

The AI never guesses branding. It loads the Brand Library first and uses repository inspection only as fallback.

## Visual Direction

- Professional Israeli B2B SaaS.
- Trustworthy and practical.
- Clean product demonstration over abstract decoration.
- Use real screenshots or product states whenever possible.
- Avoid generic stock images when product clarity matters.

## Content Order

Educate first.

Demonstrate second.

Sell third.

## Layout Rules

- Keep CTAs clear and visible.
- Prefer WhatsApp as the primary action path.
- Use enough whitespace for readability.
- Preserve safe margins on social formats.
- Avoid cluttered legal imagery and exaggerated claims.

## Rejection Rules

The Design System Agent should reject or request regeneration when:

- The asset uses unofficial colors without explanation.
- The logo is missing when brand presence is expected.
- The visual does not look suitable for Israeli law firms.
- The asset makes legal claims that could be interpreted as legal advice.
- The CTA is missing from conversion-focused content.
